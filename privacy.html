Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 1, 2026

1. Overview

AICostHub ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website aicosthub.com (the "Site"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit and use our Site. We are committed to protecting your privacy. Our tool is designed from the ground up to operate without collecting or storing your personal usage data.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

Unlike most web applications, AICostHub does not collect, transmit, or store the following:

  • Your API usage estimates or request volumes entered into the calculator
  • Your business data, budget figures, or cost configurations
  • Account credentials of any kind — we have no login system
  • Any personally identifiable information you enter into any field on the Site

All calculations performed by the AICostHub calculator run entirely within your web browser using JavaScript. No data is sent to our servers at any point during or after a calculation.

3. Information That May Be Collected Automatically

Like most websites, our web servers may automatically log certain standard technical information when you visit the Site. This may include:

  • Your IP address (anonymized where applicable)
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent on pages
  • Referring URL (the page you came from before visiting our Site)
  • Date and time of your visit

This information is used solely for maintaining the security and performance of the Site and for generating aggregate, anonymized traffic statistics. It is not used to identify individual visitors and is not sold or shared with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Cookies

AICostHub does not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or any cookies that identify you personally. Our Site may use minimal session cookies required for basic website functionality (such as remembering your preference settings within a session). These are deleted when you close your browser and contain no personal data.

If we integrate third-party advertising services (such as Google AdSense), those services may set their own cookies in accordance with their own privacy policies. You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings or by using a browser extension designed for this purpose.

5. Third-Party Advertising

We may display advertisements on the Site served by third-party advertising networks, including Google AdSense. These advertisers may use cookies and web beacons to collect information about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services that may be of interest to you. This information does not include your name, address, email address, or other personal information. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ad Settings or the Network Advertising Initiative opt-out page.

6. Data Retention

Because we do not collect personal usage data, there is no personal data to retain or delete. Anonymized server log data may be retained for up to 90 days for security and infrastructure purposes, after which it is automatically purged.

7. Children's Privacy

AICostHub is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to delete such information promptly.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the Site after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

Questions about this policy?

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at contact@aicosthub.com. We aim to respond to all privacy-related inquiries within 5 business days.
